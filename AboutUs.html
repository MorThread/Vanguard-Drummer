About Us

Vanguard Drummer was a music store and retail business that was focused in selling and repairing drum kits and accessories and cymbals. It was first started as a hobby of selling and reselling drum kits and cymbals that grown big and in late 2023 turned into a legit business retail. It is establish in order to provide support for the drumming community in the Philippines by making it more accessible for drummers to obtain or purchase necessary drumming equipment.
